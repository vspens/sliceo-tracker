# Sliceo Tracker - Client Presentation Brief

## 1) Title Slide
**Title:** Sliceo Tracker  
**Subtitle:** Turn campaign traffic into actionable lead and revenue insight  
**Context:** Client walkthrough deck

**Visual suggestion:** Dashboard hero screenshot (`screenshots/02-home-dashboard.png`)

---

## 2) The Business Problem
- Campaign clicks, leads, and delivery outcomes are usually split across tools.
- Teams struggle to answer: "Which campaign actually produced this lead?"
- Reporting is manual, slow, and often not trusted.
- Budget decisions are made with incomplete attribution context.

**Visual suggestion:** Click Stream (`screenshots/03-click-stream.png`)

---

## 3) What Sliceo Tracker Solves
- Tracks click events with campaign metadata in real time.
- Connects click journeys to leads through attribution logic.
- Monitors webhook/CRM delivery reliability end-to-end.
- Provides one source of truth for marketing + operations + leadership.

**Visual suggestion:** Home dashboard (`screenshots/02-home-dashboard.png`)

---

## 4) Step 1 - Secure Internal Access
- Private login for approved team members.
- Dashboard visibility stays internal while campaign links remain public-facing.
- Supports controlled operational usage.

**Visual suggestion:** Login (`screenshots/01-login-page.png`)

---

## 5) Step 2 - Build Clean Campaign Links
- Create short, professional campaign links (no messy UTMs in public posts).
- Define source, medium, campaign defaults.
- Keep campaign setup consistent and repeatable.

**Visual suggestion:** Link Builder (`screenshots/08-link-builder.png`)

---

## 6) Step 3 - Launch Faster With Modal Flows
- Quick modal for new tracking links.
- Quick modal for partner destination creation/update.
- Reduces setup friction during campaign launch windows.

**Visual suggestions:**  
- Create link modal (`screenshots/09-link-builder-create-modal.png`)  
- Partner modal (`screenshots/10-link-builder-partner-modal.png`)

---

## 7) Step 4 - Monitor Traffic Quality
- See who clicked (session, IP, referrer, user agent indicators).
- Detect quality differences across sources/campaigns.
- Identify weak traffic early before lead waste compounds.

**Visual suggestion:** Click Stream (`screenshots/03-click-stream.png`)

---

## 8) Step 5 - Connect Leads to Campaign Journeys
- Leads are matched to click journeys with attribution logic.
- Supports first-touch, last-touch, and assist-style insight.
- Gives teams context for why a lead converted, not just that it converted.

**Visual suggestion:** Leads (`screenshots/04-leads.png`)

---

## 9) Step 6 - Validate Delivery Reliability
- Every push attempt is logged with status and response.
- Quickly identify failed deliveries and integration issues.
- Protect pipeline quality and prevent silent lead loss.

**Visual suggestion:** Deliveries (`screenshots/05-deliveries.png`)

---

## 10) Step 7 - Campaign-Level Decisioning
- Campaign Insights compares performance across source/medium/campaign.
- Understand contribution via first-touch, last-touch, and assist context.
- Use data to reallocate budget toward high-performing campaigns.

**Visual suggestion:** Campaign Insights (`screenshots/06-campaign-insights.png`)

---

## 11) Step 8 - Deep Campaign Drilldown
- Dedicated screen for one campaign:
  - click event forensics
  - top referrers
  - linked leads
  - CSV export for reporting
- Enables practical diagnosis and optimization.

**Visual suggestion:** Campaign detail (`screenshots/07-campaign-detail.png`)

---

## 12) Real-World Value Scenario #1 (Optimization)
- LinkedIn campaign gets high clicks but weak lead quality.
- Drilldown reveals weak referrer mix and poor conversion profile.
- Team adjusts targeting + landing narrative.
- Result: stronger conversion quality in following cycle.

**Visual suggestion:** Campaign detail (`screenshots/07-campaign-detail.png`)

---

## 13) Real-World Value Scenario #2 (Operational Risk)
- Leads are generated, but some never reach CRM.
- Delivery logs reveal webhook failures.
- Team fixes endpoint/auth config quickly.
- Result: reduced lead loss and improved trust in reporting.

**Visual suggestion:** Deliveries (`screenshots/05-deliveries.png`)
